Delete Your Moodly Account

Your privacy matters to us. If you'd like to delete your Moodly account and associated data, follow the instructions below.

🔐 What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your account details (email address, UID) will be permanently deleted.
  • All mood logs, journal entries, and analytics data associated with your account will be erased from our Firebase database.
  • We retain no personally identifiable information after deletion.

📩 How to Request Deletion

Send an email to contact@bytebrim.com with the subject:
Delete Moodly Account

Include your registered email address or UID (if known). We will verify the request and process the deletion within 7 days.

🕒 Retention Timeline

We retain backup logs for up to 7 days after deletion. After that, the data is permanently erased.

If you have any concerns or require assistance, contact us at contact@bytebrim.com.
